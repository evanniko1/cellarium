# Cellarium's Socratic Council vs the K-Dense reasoning skills

A deliberate comparison of our bespoke Council against the four K-Dense skills whose remit overlaps it —
`consciousness-council`, `hypothesis-generation`, `scientific-critical-thinking`, `scientific-brainstorming` —
to justify keeping our own and to name what's worth borrowing.

## What our Socratic Council is

Proposer → Skeptic → Judge over a fixed loop that turns a user question into a **falsifiable, operationalized
hypothesis**: a claim + H1/H0, operational definitions onto real instrument channels, a **single decisive test**
(the falsifier), enumerated rivals with distinguishing results, ceteris-paribus assumptions, and queue-ready
candidate designs. Four properties make it what it is:

1. **Blind to the corpus (the quarantine/recitation control).** Roles see only the instrument's capability labels
   and the question — never a reading. This is the scientific control the paper rests on.
2. **Instrument-grounded.** Every operational definition must land on a channel the model actually computes; a
   design must be inside the validated envelope.
3. **Decisive-test discipline.** Convergence requires a falsifier that can actually fail and that *discriminates*
   the named rivals — enforced by a convergence gate + a quota of substantive objections + per-objection resolution.
4. **Reproducible + ablatable.** Fixed roles, pinned temperature, leak-control ablation — built for a paper, not a chat.

## The four overlapping skills (from their SKILL.md)

| Skill | What it is | Input → output | Blind? | Grounded to an instrument? | Single decisive test? |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **consciousness-council** | A general "Mind Council": 4–6 clashing *archetypes* (Architect, Contrarian, Empiricist, Ethicist…) synthesized into a view on **any** question/decision | question → multi-perspective synthesis | No | No | No |
| **hypothesis-generation** | Structured hypotheses **from observations/data** → predictions, mechanisms, experiment design; mandates figures | data/observations → hypotheses | **No — starts from the data** | No | No (a set, not one) |
| **scientific-critical-thinking** | Grade **existing** evidence via GRADE / Cochrane Risk-of-Bias — biases, confounders, validity | a paper/claim → quality assessment | No | No | n/a (a reviewer) |
| **scientific-brainstorming** | Open-ended divergent ideation, interdisciplinary connections, research gaps; pre-observation | topic → ideas | No | No | No |

## The core differences

- **Blindness.** None of the four are blind — and one (`hypothesis-generation`) is explicitly the *opposite*: it
  frames *from* observations. Our Council frames *before* seeing any corpus result. Swapping in any of these would
  destroy the recitation control that makes Cellarium publishable. This alone is decisive.
- **Convergence to ONE decisive test.** The skills produce *diversity* (`consciousness-council`, `brainstorming`),
  a *set* of hypotheses (`hypothesis-generation`), or a *critique* (`critical-thinking`). Ours converges — under a
  formal gate — to a single falsifier that discriminates its rivals. That's the whole point of a falsifiable hypothesis.
- **Instrument grounding.** The skills are platform-agnostic prose. Ours is welded to a specific model's channels
  and validated envelope, so what it proposes is *runnable here*.
- **Role shape.** `consciousness-council` rotates 4–6 archetypes for breadth; ours is 3 fixed roles for depth and
  reproducibility. Breadth is cheap; the disciplined Proposer/Skeptic/Judge with a resolution ledger is the value.

## What we keep vs borrow

**Keep (do not replace):** our blind, instrument-grounded, gate-converged Council. It is the differentiator and the
paper's control; none of the four skills carry blindness, grounding, or decisive-test convergence.

**Worth borrowing (as enhancements, not replacements):**

- **From `scientific-critical-thinking` → GRADE / Cochrane ROB.** Not for the (blind) Skeptic, but for **Cellwright's
  literature reconciliation**: when comparing a grounded sim result to published evidence, grade that evidence's
  quality rather than treating every citation as equal. High value, low risk.
- **From `experimental-design` (already vendored) → real DOE.** Randomization/blocking/factorial for the Council's
  falsifier panels — beyond the current seeds×generations.
- **From `consciousness-council` → rotating archetype lenses.** Optionally diversify the Skeptic's objections with a
  couple of archetype prompts. Risk: dilutes the falsifier focus, so gated + experimental only.

**Explicitly not adopting:** `consciousness-council` / `hypothesis-generation` / `scientific-brainstorming` as the
Council. They are excellent *general* research aids; ours is a *controlled scientific instrument*. Different jobs.
